Branding and Concept

Our brand, "Track the Fox," symbolizes the cunning and elusiveness of our challenges, as well as the skillful pursuit required by our participants. The fox represents the hidden information and clever adversaries that OSINT investigators must outsmart.

Our color scheme of deep blues and vibrant orange represents the contrast between the hidden (blue) and the revealed (orange) in the world of intelligence gathering.

Series Structure

The Track the Fox series consists of 5 initial challenges, released bi-weekly. Each challenge is designed to test different aspects of OSINT gathering and analysis.

  1. Week 1-2: The Encrypted Enigma - Focused on decryption, metadata analysis, and digital forensics
  2. Week 3-4: The Vanishing Vlogger - Emphasis on social media investigation and geolocation techniques
  3. Week 5-6: Corporate Crypto Conundrum - Delving into financial OSINT and blockchain analysis
  4. Week 7-8: The Sound of Secrets - Exploring audio analysis and steganography
  5. Week 9-10: Digital Breadcrumbs - Advanced techniques in correlating disparate online identities

Challenge Components

  • Detailed backstory and scenario
  • Multiple data points across various platforms
  • Technical elements requiring tool usage and analysis
  • Red herrings to simulate real-world complexity
  • Scoring system based on accuracy, speed, and methodology
  • Community forum for discussions and hints

Challenge #1: The Encrypted Enigma - Full Detail Plan

Storyline

A cybersecurity researcher named Alex, known for uncovering cyber threats, has gone dark. Their last known activity was an encrypted blog post hinting at a major discovery. Participants must piece together various technical clues left in metadata, logs, and digital artifacts to find out what happened to Alex and reveal their discovery.

Participants will be scored based on accuracy, speed, and the ability to navigate through red herrings. Bonus points for identifying and explaining sophisticated techniques used in the solution.
