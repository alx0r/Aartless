Storyline

A cybersecurity researcher named Alex, known for uncovering cyber threats, has gone dark. Their last known activity was an encrypted blog post hinting at a major discovery. Participants must piece together various technical clues left in metadata, logs, and digital artifacts to find out what happened to Alex and reveal their discovery.
